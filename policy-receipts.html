Policy Receipts

What does a policy *actually* do? CivHub's Policy Receipts tool turns complex legislation and government proposals into clear, readable summaries — just like a receipt.

How It Works

  • Paste a policy name or summary – e.g., “Zoning Reform Act” or “Rent Stabilization Law”
  • We pull from public data – federal, state, or local sources
  • You get a simple, source-linked receipt – who pays, who benefits, what it changes

If we can’t generate a receipt, we’ll tell you why — and invite you to contribute trusted source files to grow our public ledger. Because transparency starts with participation.
