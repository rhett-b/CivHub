CivHub: A Civic Interface

CivHub is a public trust tool. We turn complex government data into clear, contextual, actionable insight.

We're a platform built for clarity, not clicks. A tool for comprehension, not an algorithmicly-driven platform for engagement

🧾 Generate a Policy Receipt 📊 Explore Civic Insights 📝 Read Ledger Notes

Why CivHub?

  • Because you can’t participate in what you can’t understand
  • Because public data is a public good
  • Because trust begins with comprehension
  • Because civic systems should reflect lived reality

Currently Available

🧾 Policy Receipts
AI-generated summaries of what legislation actually does, with links to real sources.

📊 Civic Insights
Data-backed snapshots of housing, labor, infrastructure, and governance systems.

📝 Ledger Notes
Short and long-form commentary on policy clarity, civic design, and digital governance.
