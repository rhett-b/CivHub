About CivHub

CivHub is built on the idea that transparent, accessible data is a core component of a functioning democracy. We're creating an AI-first civic platform that lets everyday citizens engage with their governments in ways never before possible.

Core goals:

  • Transparency and open access to government data
  • Accountability through AI-driven insights
  • Decentralization and resistance to corruption
