About CivHub

CivHub is an open-source civic platform designed to make public systems readable — to build civic trust through transparency, design, and data.

Our Mission

We believe that public data is a public good, and that you can’t participate in what you can’t understand. CivHub is built on the belief that good governance begins with good design.

What We’ve Built

Our current platform includes:

  • Insights: Bite-sized findings on budgets, housing, labor, and more.
  • Ledger Notes: Deeper reflections, hypotheses, and commentary.
  • Policy Receipts (beta): A tool to generate easy-to-read summaries of policy impacts using real data.

Where We’re Going

Our long-term vision is to build a civic operating system — a stack of tools for data legibility, public comprehension, and meaningful participation. From AI-assisted policy simulations to citizen-generated datasets, CivHub aims to evolve how we govern in the digital age.
